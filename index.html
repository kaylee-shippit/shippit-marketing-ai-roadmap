The endgame
Remove the low-leverage work between functions.
The business will ask: "How is Marketing getting more efficient with AI?" The answer isn't a list of tools — it's a system where AI handles the hand-offs: the reformatting, the starting-from-scratch, the manual coordination between Research, Content, and Distribution. Every function redeployes to the work that actually moves pipeline.
✓ Already proven with Delivered Primary research → content → multi-channel distribution → signal capture. Running in a 2-week cycle, without AI. This system scales and automates that model across all of marketing.
